What is NeuralHacks?

NeuralHacks is a hackathon taking place in the Bay Area where high school students get the oppurtunity to create their own projects centered around Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. AI is at the forefront of technology that is shaping the world around us. NeuralHacks provides students the chance to learn about and develop their own AI programs that could have their own impact on the world.
